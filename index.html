The Inception


"Most People miss great opportunities because of their misperception of time. Don’t Wait! The time will never be just right."
This is your chance to use your skills to find innovative and exciting solutions to real-world problems! Hack Club BVP, Techshuttle presents to you Inception 2021.
Inception will be an event like none other. With a Hackathon, Ideathon, Treasure Hunt and a Competitive Coding Contest, Inception will challenge the intellect of the participants. Aimed at stimulating innovation, cultivating a technical temper among the student community, and promoting healthy competition among peers. A week long event that aims to bring together participants from across India and enhance the diversity of our community. For all the tech geeks out there, we assure to conduct the event with exceptional spirit, reach and opportunities for everyone.
Put forth innovative solutions, initiate new discussions, expand your skills and compete with the sharpest of minds in a one of a kind event, with great prizes at stake.
